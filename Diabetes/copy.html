      COVID-19 self-assessment

      Last updated: July 18, 2022

      Take this self-assessment if you were exposed to COVID-19 (novel coronavirus), have symptoms or for screening.

      You will get a recommendation on what to do next.

      For workplace screening purposes you can print off or email your results to your employer.

      You can also take it on behalf of someone else.

      If this is a medical emergency, call 911. Advise them of your symptoms and if you have recently travelled.

      Everyone in your household must stay home if anyone has COVID-19 symptoms or is waiting for test results after experiencing symptoms.

      Stay home while the symptomatic household member is isolating.

      The self-assessment is only meant as an aid and cannot diagnose you. Anyone who is sick or has any symptoms of illness, including those not listed in this screening tool, should stay home and seek assessment from a health care provider if needed. Consult a health care provider if you have medical questions.
